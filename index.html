How to Find Coordinates in PDF Files

Finding coordinates in PDF files is essential for developers working with PDF forms, document automation, and interactive PDFs. This free online PDF coordinate finder tool makes it easy to get the exact X and Y coordinates of any point on your PDF pages.

What Are PDF Coordinates?

PDF coordinates represent the position of elements on a page using an X and Y axis system. The origin point starts at the bottom-left corner of each page, with X values increasing to the right and Y values increasing upward. Understanding these coordinates is crucial for accurately positioning form fields, signatures, stamps, and other elements.
